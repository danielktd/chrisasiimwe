Chris Asiimwe is a renown author, public speaker, entrepreneur and one of the founders of KLIN Entertainment LLC- The Leading Gospel Music record Label.

His life philosophy is built around helping people around the world discover their true potential.
Chris also believes that all humans can maximise their full potential by activating the required principles highlighted in his books and other materials.
Upon being completely delievered from addictions, Chris Asiimwe set out on a mission to help other young people who are struggling with addictions. He is also a Christian and advocates for high moral principles and standards.
